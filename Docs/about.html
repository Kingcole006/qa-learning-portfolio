Who I Am

A little background

I am a QA Automation Engineer in training based in Sugar Hill, GA. I do not just follow tutorials. I build real projects, debug real problems, and document everything like a professional engineer would.


Over the past 2 months I have gone from writing my first test case to building a complete TypeScript/Playwright framework with Page Object Model, custom fixtures, data-driven testing, API tests, cross-browser testing, test tagging, a live CI/CD pipeline, and SQL database validation queries used by QA engineers on the job.


Before tech, I spent 3+ years as a Licensed Insurance Claims Adjuster at Allstate and State Farm. That background sharpened my root cause analysis, documentation discipline, and ability to work under pressure -- skills that transfer directly into QA engineering.
